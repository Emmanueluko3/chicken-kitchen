ABOUT US

CHICKEN KITCHEN

Chicken Kitchen is a Nigerian fast-food chain and franchise that specializes in chicken recipes, especially fried chicken. It was founded by Emmanuel Stephen, the first restaurant was established in Maitama, Abuja in 2022.

Chicken Kitchen is headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. The company is a subsidiary of Food Concepts Plc, a Nigerian food based company.

The company is arguably Nigeria's largest chicken restaurant chain with over 40 outlets in Abuja and over 70 outlets nationwide. Chicken Kitchen has also expanded services to other West African countries including Ghana
